Enthropy Systems

Invariant Limits of Complex Systems

Independent research into structural constraints on global consistency, degradation, and cyclic behavior in large-scale systems.

What this is

Enthropy Systems is an independent research effort focused on identifying invariant constraints that govern the behavior of large-scale, heterogeneous systems.

What this is not

This is not a product company, consultancy, or applied engineering firm. Applications are used solely to establish enablement and generality.

Current focus

Mapping and protecting IP around invariant-based system architectures, supported by extensive simulations and formal (Lean 4) reasoning.

Status

Work is ongoing. Selected discussion occur privately.
